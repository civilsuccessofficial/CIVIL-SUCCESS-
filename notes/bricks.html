📘 Introduction

A brick is an artificial building material made by moulding clay into rectangular blocks and burning them in a kiln. Bricks are one of the oldest and most widely used construction materials because they are strong, durable, economical and easily available.

📚 Definition

A brick is a rectangular block of burnt clay used in masonry construction for walls, foundations, columns and other structural works.

✅ Advantages

  • High durability
  • Good compressive strength
  • Fire resistant
  • Economical
  • Easily available
  • Easy construction

❌ Disadvantages

  • Low tensile strength
  • Heavy weight
  • High water absorption
  • Construction is slower than blocks

🏗 Uses of Bricks

  • Building walls
  • Foundations
  • Columns
  • Arches
  • Pavements
  • Boundary walls

🎯 SSC JE Important Points

  • First Class Bricks are used for superior construction.
  • Water absorption should not exceed 20% by weight.
